Beginner Method

The simplest way to solve a 3x3 cube is following these steps:

  1. Make a cross on one side.
  2. Solve the corners of that same side.
  3. Solve the edges of the middle layer.
  4. Make a cross on the top layer.
  5. Match the top layer edges.
  6. Solve the corners of the top layer.

This is easier said than done! To help with these steps, use this online resource.
