Welcome to the start of your puzzle solving career!

The Rubik's Cube brought twisty puzzles to life in the 1980s, and there have been variations created and released for puzzle lovers to enjoy since.

Our Mission

This website was created to help puzzle lovers find solving guides and new puzzles to challenge themselves. Whether you are looking to learn how to solve a Rubik's cube for the first time or dipping your toes into the world of speed solving, we hope to provide the proper resources to further your progress.

My Journey

I first learned to solve a Rubik's cube in my eighth grade science class thanks to the help of my teacher. I was beyond fascinated with memorizing patterns and algorithms (sequence of moves) and continued to learn more about solving as I continued to high school. Thanks to the help of online forums and YouTube videos (much less content in 2010), I learned more advanced methods and started collecting different puzzles of various shapes and sizes.
